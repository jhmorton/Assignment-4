The sights of Fatih, Istanbul






In regards to sightseeing, it is the mightiest city

But why? I've called out Fatih here, which is equivalent to a borough like Brooklyn in NYC, because this is the oldest, most famed part of the city. It has things like the Hagia Sophia and Topkapi Palace.

I'd also like to reiterate that I've never been the let's-go-to-the-tourist-places type, but I really couldn't help myself on this trip.
