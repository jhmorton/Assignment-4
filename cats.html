Cats I met in Istanbul











Seriously, there's a ton of cats in Istanbul

But why? Well, the common opinion ties the respect and reverence given cats to the belief that the Prophet Muhammed was once saved from the bite of a venomous snake but a watchful feline, as discussed on Muslim Heritage.

As Turkey is a predominantly Muslim country, the cats are afforded great liberty and no one seems to pay them much mind.
